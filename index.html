About Me

Hello! I'm Yugao Zhu (朱余高), a third-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Electronic and Computer Engineering at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), advised by Prof. Zhiyao Xie.

My research lies in hypergraph partitioning — developing efficient, scalable algorithms and frameworks for large-scale chip design automation. I am also broadly interested in the application of large language models (LLMs) to EDA and other domains.

Prior to HKUST, I received my Bachelor's degree from the Department of Electronic Engineering at Tsinghua University.
